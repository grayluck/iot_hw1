Android

Step 1

Open Android Studio and create a new blank Android project. The project looks like the following screenshot.

Step 2

Modify the property of TextView. Change the color of the text by adding android:textColor="#66f"

 attribute to TextView. Add background by adding android:background="#aaa" to Text View. Change the size of the TextView by android:textSize="30sp". Also, change the alignment and widith to fill the parent while remain centered by adding attributes android:layout_width="0dp" and android:textAlignment="center". Move the TextView by dragging it around to change its position.

Step 3

Similarly, add another TextView for fun.

Step 4

Modify the title of Action Bar, from dummy iot_hw1 to a customized title. It is done by adding android:label="I also modified the title!" to the activity definition in AndroidManifest.xml.

Step 5

Create a fancyBackground.xml, with a linear definition of gradient background style.

Step 6

Apply fancyBackground.xml to the background of the layout of the activity by adding android:background="@drawable/fancyBackground"

 to layout/activity_main.xml.

Step 7

Connect my Android device and test the project with it.
